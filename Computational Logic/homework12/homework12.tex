  \begin{displaymath}
    (K (p \implies q)) \implies (\neg K \neg p \implies \neg K \neg q).
  \end{displaymath}

  modal inference rules, and \verb+\open[K]+ to introduce a modal
  context.  You may use derived rules of inference, including Modus
  Tollens (\verb+\mt+).
